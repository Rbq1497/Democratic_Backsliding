Horizontal and Diagonal Accountability Indices: Global Averages

  1. Observations about Government Powers and Institutional constraints

Horizontal Accountability

  • Legislatures have become weaker since 2012

  • Judiciaries became stronger from 2014-2016, and have sharply weakened from 2016 until now

Diagonal Accountability

  • Independent auditing and review agencies (NGOs) increased their oversight powers from 2014 -2018, and have been weakened since

  • Civil Society constraints on government powers have declined since 2012

  • Freedom from Government surveillance has steadily decreased since 2012

  1. Observations about institutional corruption

Horizontal accountability

  • Corruption in the executive has increased since 2013

  • Corruption in the judicial branch decreased from 2014-2017; increased from 2017 to 2019, and has plateau until 2022

  • Corruption in the legislature has increased since 2014
